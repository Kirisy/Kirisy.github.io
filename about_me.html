About me

I am a digital illustrator and author of the dayx comic series. I've been doing digital drawing since 2012, and I have extensive knowledge of applications such as Photoshop, Procreate and clip studio. I enjoy drawing characters, stories, book covers and so on.
Since 2021 I've been releasing my own story on webtoon called DayX. It's a story about the future after a massive disaster and the people who had to find a way to keep surviving and evolving in the remaining territories.
If you're interested, you can check out my gallery!
